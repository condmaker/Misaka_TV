Urgent Quests & Concerts: August 2020 - Part 1

Your schedule is going to be action-packed Arks, because several Dark Falz are stirring and ARKS HQ has implemented a new Urgent Quest–Beach Wars! See this article for more information!

Clear your schedules, operatives, because your “to-do” list is about to get a lot longer! These next few weeks will have you building strength in the sand in Beach Wars as well as subduing Dark Falz Elder and Dark Falz Luther. No one ever said life as an ARKS Operative would be easy, but at least it’s never boring! Should you need further information on Urgent Quests and how to prepare for them, see this page.

 

Quna is back in action! Concerts can inspire operatives to greater heights of aptitude on subsequent quests or missions that they undertake. In addition, some Arks also report getting luckier in finding powerful items. ARKS HQ strongly recommends that operatives attend these performances to ensure they’re at their fighting best when facing off against the Falspawn threat. Also, remember to ask Quna for an “Encore” when her song finishes!

MINING BASE DEFENSE OPERATIONS: ENDING

Duration (time allowed to complete quest): Until victory, or the destruction of all ARKS base sites (Repeatable, a total of 6 waves)

A strong Falspawn signal, with an orientation suggesting that it originates from Lillipa, has been received by ARKS HQ. Early theories suggest it could be Apprentice the Vernal attempting to bolster the Falspawn forces assaulting the Mining Base. Caution will be necessary during the approach, and ARKS HQ has deemed this a good opportunity to employ an underground exploration craft to approach the target with stealth. Equip yourselves accordingly, then head to the Quest Counter with all due haste.


 


BARREN BLOSSOM, DESTROYER OF ALL

Duration (time allowed to complete quest): 60 Minutes (30-minute window to access quest)

Attention, all members of ARKS. ARKS Support System. Dark Falz Gemini has formed an unstable plane above one of our Landing Craft, and your assistance is urgently needed. He seems to have manifested as a large structure, and is intent upon damaging our fleet and hindering our progress towards what can only be the source of the evil and corruption. We must defeat Dark Falz Gemini to have a chance at achieving our foremost goal; ensuring the destruction of Profound Darkness.





THE MANIFESTED PLANETBREAKER

Duration (time allowed to complete quest): 30 Minutes (Repeatable, up to 4 times)

ARKS HQ has pin-pointed abnormal readings originating from the planet Harukotan. The Harbinger of Calamity has assumed a new form, and should it succeed in breaching the defensive barrier…well, suffice it to say that the magnitude of the resulting devastation is unfathomable. Fleet Oracle will need every valiant ARKS Operative to suit up and ship out with all due haste to neutralize this threat. Operatives are encouraged to check their supplies of recovery items before boarding a Gateway Ship.


 


THE CHANT TO CLEANSE THE CALAMITY (Sequel)

Duration (time allowed to complete quest): 7 Minutes (Players must complete “The Manifested Planetbreaker” to gain access to this Urgent Quest)

Sukunahime has partially succeeded in her effort to save Harukotan by entrapping a part of the Harbinger of Calamity within the Endsong Realm. This part of the Harbinger must be kept occupied while its spirit is purified during the imprisonment. To aid Arks in diverting the Harbinger from focusing on escape, modified AIS units with a limited range will be provided. The cooperation of all Arks is needed to neutralize this dangerous manifestation.

 




CHAOS, RENDER OF TRANQUILITY

Duration (time allowed to complete quest): 20 Minutes, or collect 1000 Quest Points (Repeatable)

Attention all operatives! We have detected an infiltration of Sylvants, Automata, and Draconians, as well as Falspawn, in the City Area of the ARKS Ship. The presence of enemies thought not to associate with Falspawn is troubling as it is possible, they are in the early stages of corruption but aren’t displaying the physical characteristics typically associated with the infection. There is no time for investigation, however, and all invaders are being classified as “kill on sight” targets.




THE RAGING OBSIDIAN ARM

Duration (time allowed to complete quest): 20 Minutes (Repeatable)

Attention Arks! We appear to be under a blitz attack from a fragment of Elder the Gargantuan that is approaching at great speed. Initial analysis indicates that this fragment is atypical of those previously encountered. All Arks are to quickly arm themselves for a much tougher fight with the goal of intercepting and weakening the fragment to provide time for full-scale resources to be deployed.




ELDER OF THE UNFATHOMABLE ABYSS (SEQUEL)

Duration (time allowed to complete quest): 20 Minutes (Players must complete “The Raging Obsidian Arm” to gain access to this Urgent Quest)

Earlier attempts to weaken the fragment of Elder the Gargantuan appear to have only strengthened it, and all Arks are required to prepare for what could be the final battle for ARKS. However, operatives are encouraged to remember that their combined effort and passion cannot but lead to victory. Take comfort in the strength of your comrades and prepare for the battle to come.




THE CUNNING BLACK-WINGED VANGUARD

Duration (time allowed to complete quest): 20 Minutes (Repeatable)

Your immediate attention is needed, Arks! The Mothership has been corrupted and is moving to intercept the main fleet of Oracle. As this course runs countermand to previous navigational orders, it is believed that the helm has been taken over by Dark Falz and his minions. Operatives are to prepare for an all-out attempt to retake what is ours, eradicate all Falspawn, and locate Dark Falz.


 



THE FALSE CHAMPION: LUTHER THE FALLEN (Sequel)

Duration (time allowed to complete quest): 30 Minutes (Players must complete "The Cunning Black Winged Vanguard" to gain access to this Urgent Quest)

The readings of corruption from the Mothership have increased in amplitude, indicating that Dark Falz Luther is aboard. He is familiar with the layout of the Ship, so Arks should be wary of traps and ambushing Falspawn. The outcome of the final battle is in the hands of our brave operatives, and we have no doubt they shall triumph.


 

Prepare yourselves accordingly, and make sure to sleep lightly operatives! See you at the Quest Counter, Arks!
